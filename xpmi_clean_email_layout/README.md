# XPMI - Clean Email Layout

## Overview

- Adds a company flag to simplify outgoing email notifications.
- When enabled, it hides the document title block, the company contact line, and the "Powered by Odoo" footer.
- The company logo and company name remain visible.

## Configuration

- Settings > Companies > your company
- Enable: "Nascondi header e footer email"

## Notes

- Applies to the standard notification layout and the light layout used by delivery emails.
